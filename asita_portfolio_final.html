About

A story of passion and purpose

"With a strong drive to make a positive change in the world."

Asita Darusalam Rahardja is a graduating senior at Kolese Kanisius Jakarta — one of Indonesia's most prestigious Jesuit schools. Known for his academic excellence and extraordinary leadership, Asita carries a remarkable record spanning debate championships, international MUN diplomacy, environmental advocacy, and community impact across Jakarta and beyond.

Having been recognized as Valedictorian from junior high, holding Summa Cum Laude honors throughout senior high, and founding multiple organizations — from CaniMUNDO to CANVAS SDGs — Asita represents a generation of Indonesian youth determined to lead with both intellect and heart.
